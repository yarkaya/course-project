About us

Litka Music Vocal Studio is a high class music studio founded in
2014 and based in Lviv, Uktaine. This studio is recommended for
adults, because of difficult vocal technics which are
too incomprehensible for children. LMVS team
is coaching and preparing singers for:

auditions in Music Conservatory

showcases for record labels

recording sessions

1 year course

This is an intensive
course, which duration
is one school year. The
course provides 3 vocal
lessons a week. Songs
are performed in class
with backing tracks or
live guitar and each
class begins with
breathing and vocal
warmups on acoustic piano.
