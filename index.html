About us

Litka Music Vocal Studio is a high class music studio
founded in 2014 and based in Lviv, Uktaine. This studio
is recommended for adults, because of difficult vocal
technics which are too incomprehensible for children.
LMVS team is coaching and preparing singers for:

auditions in Music Conservatory
